Race numbers detection and recognition from photos

A tools for easier way to detect numbers on images. Allow quickly tagging thousands of photos from a sport event. Save your time on manual tagging and make photos searchable at the same day.

What is it?

Imagine, you have just completed your first marathon distance and would like to share the success with your friends. The best way to do it is sharing pictures with you.

A marathon organizers, sports events photography companies or just funs make thousands of photos.

How to find a photo with the particular participant? The easiest way is to search by his race number.

We create the service that allows detect bibs on photos and recognize numbers.

How does it work?

Service works as web service and API. It developed by using computer vision technologies.

There are two ways to use it:

  • By subscription (once/ by month/ by year).

    You upload your photos at our website and get information for each file what race numbers it contains.

  • Use API and implement it at you site (fee depends on number of participants of you event and possible adaptation for you events).

Where are we now?

We developed a prototype. If you would like to try it, send us the following information:

  • Who you are (a photographer/a sports events photography company/ a sport event organizers).

  • Tell us about your case of usage.

  • Format of race numbers.

  • Your collection of photos.
